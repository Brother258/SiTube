Screen Interaction:

  • Hide Select Video Button By clicking three times anywhere on the screen hide "Select Video" button for an unobstructed viewing experience.
  • Adjust Playback Speed Hover 0-40% (left side) for speed control.
  • Skip 5s Forward/Rewind Hover 41-60% (middle) to skip 5s.
  • Control Volume Hover 61-100% (right side) for sound control.

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Alt + n or Ctrl + z: Open file selection dialog.
  • d Toggle the playlist sidebar.
  • k or Space: Toggle play/pause.
  • t: Show/hide video details overlay.
  • f: Toggle full-screen mode.
  • i: Toggle Picture-in-Picture mode.
  • ArrowRight: Forward 5 seconds.
  • ArrowLeft: Rewind 5 seconds.
  • ArrowUp: Increase volume by 10%.
  • ArrowDown: Decrease volume by 10%.
  • [: Adjust playback speed by -0.1.
  • ]: Adjust playback speed by +0.1.
  • Backspace: Reset playback speed to normal.
  • Ctrl + c: Toggle video controls.
  • Shift + c: Toggle clock display.

Use these keyboard and mouse shortcuts to control SiTube effortlessly and enjoy a seamless video-watching experience!
